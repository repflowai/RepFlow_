THE FLOW

Three steps.
Fully automatic.

01

Remind

RepFlow watches your calendar and sends reminders at 48 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours before each appointment — timed to your clinic's local timezone, not ours.

02

Confirm or cancel

Patients tap one button — no phone tag, no waiting on hold. Their response is captured before they're ever due at your front desk.

Refill

A cancellation updates your calendar and notifies your team instantly, so the slot can be rebooked before it's ever sat empty.
